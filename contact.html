Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity?

Minimum order quantities vary by product. Contact us for specific requirements and we'll work with you to find a solution that fits your needs.

Do you provide samples?

Yes, we provide samples for qualified buyers. Sample costs and shipping are typically covered by the buyer, but may be credited toward future orders.

What certifications do you have?

We hold GlobalG.A.P, Organic, Fair Trade, and Carbon Neutral certifications. All products meet EU and US import requirements.

How do I become a supplier farmer?

We're always looking for committed farmers. Contact us to learn about our training programs, certification requirements, and application process.

What is your shipping timeline?

Fresh produce typically ships within 2-3 days of harvest. Processed products have varying lead times. We provide detailed timelines with each quote.

Do you offer investment opportunities?

Yes, we have several investment programs including plot sponsorship and revenue sharing. Visit our Investment page for more details.
