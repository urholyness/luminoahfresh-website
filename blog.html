Meet Violet – A Single Mother Managing 3 Acres for Export

March 15, 2024 By Grace Wanjiku 8 min read

Violet Cherotich transformed her small plot in Uasin Gishu County into a thriving export operation, becoming a role model for women farmers across Kenya. Her journey from subsistence farming to international markets shows the power of training, technology, and community support.

"When I started with GreenStem three years ago, I was barely feeding my family," Violet shares, standing among rows of vibrant French beans. "Now, my children are in school, and I'm teaching other women how to farm for export."
